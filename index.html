Who We Are

Turning compassion into lasting change

Founded in the heart of Chhattisgarh, InAmigos Foundation has grown into a nationally-recognized force for social good. We run six flagship programs spanning education, nutrition, animal welfare, women's empowerment, environmental conservation, and skill development — touching lives in 28 states.

Every rupee donated is accountable. Our transparent governance, backed by government certifications and ISO standards, ensures your trust is never misplaced.

Our 6 Programs

Where your support goes

Six focused programs. One shared mission: equity, dignity, and opportunity for every Indian.

📚
Education
Project BachpanSala

Free schooling and learning materials for underprivileged children. Our classrooms have enrolled 12,000+ kids across rural India.

Learn more →
🍛
Nutrition
Project Seva

Daily meals, clothing drives, and essential supplies delivered to homeless individuals and flood-affected families.

Learn more →
🐾
Animal Welfare
Project Jeev

Rescue, medical care, and shelter for stray and injured animals. 2,000+ animals treated and rehomed since 2021.

Learn more →
👩
Women Empowerment
Project Udaan

Vocational training, legal awareness camps, and micro-finance linkage for women in tribal and semi-urban communities.

Learn more →
🌿
Environment
Project Prakriti

Tree plantation drives, plastic-free campaigns, and watershed management. 50,000+ saplings planted across 12 districts.

Learn more →
💼
Skill Development
Project Vikas

Free technical and soft-skills training aligned with NSDC standards, helping youth secure formal employment.
